To date, the album has amassed in excess of 100 million in sales worldwide.</p>\n<div class=\"PRN_ImbeddedAssetReference\" id=\"DivAssetPlaceHolder1\">\n<p>                        <a href=\"https://mma.prnewswire.com/media/1817207/Michael_Jackson_Thriller_40_Cover_Artwork.html\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"nofollow noopener\"><br />\n<img src=\"https://mma.prnewswire.com/media/1817207/Michael_Jackson_Thriller_40_Cover_Artwork.jpg\" title=\"Michael Jackson Thriller 40 Cover Artwork\" alt=\"Michael Jackson Thriller 40 Cover Artwork\"><br />\n</a></p>\n</div>\n<p>In the US, Walmart will have an exclusive version of the original <i>Thriller </i>album&nbsp;with an alternate 40<sup>th</sup> anniversary cover, while Target will have an exclusive version of the original album with a commemorative <i>Thriller 40</i> vinyl slip mat. Both of these will also be available outside the US from various retailers.</p>\n<p>Mastered from the original analog master tapes, Mobile Fidelity&nbsp;will also make available the original <b><i>Thriller</i></b> album as a One-Step 180g 33RPM LP, pressed at RTI and strictly limited to 40,000 numbered copies as well as a hybrid SACD. (An UltraDisc One-Step 180g 45RPM 2LP set of&nbsp;<i>Thriller </i>will be released at a future date.)</p>\n<p>Go&nbsp;<a href=\"https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&amp;l=en&amp;o=3535819-1&amp;h=3470105182&amp;u=https%3A%2F%2Feur01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com%2F%3Furl%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fmichaeljackson.lnk.to%252FThriller40th%26data%3D05%257C01%257Cmaria.malta%2540sonymusic.com%257C4085d2ec15ef45785b3108da339ed26f%257Cf0aff3b791a54aaeaf71c63e1dda2049%257C0%257C0%257C637879054470814013%257CUnknown%257CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%253D%257C3000%257C%257C%257C%26sdata%3Dt1HVNMucNkENfgqiXt04cNExmeAr%252FzZvz1CpnPmSMS4%253D%26reserved%3D0&amp;a=HERE\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"nofollow noopener\">HERE</a> for information and to Pre-Order.</p>\n<p>Several activations are planned between now and the end of the year to honor Michael&#8217;s epic creation which won a record setting 8 Grammys, smashed musical barriers and changed the frontiers of pop music and videos forever.&nbsp; <span class=\"xn-person\">Michael Jackson&#8217;s</span> <b><i>Thriller</i></b> has spent more than 500 weeks on the Billboard album chart and has sold over 100 million albums since its release on <span class=\"xn-chron\">November 30, 1982</span>. The first such activation is the first drop of new merchandise featuring the special Thriller 40 logo which is now available exclusively through the MichaelJackson.com webstore.</p>\n<p><b><i><u>MORE ABOUT THRILLER:</u></i></b></p>\n<p><b><i>Thriller</i></b>&nbsp;holds a modern-day record of 37 weeks at No. 1 on Billboard. It was the first album in history to spend each of its first 80 weeks in the album chart&#8217;s Top 10, a feat only reached by one other album in the nearly four decades since. During its 112<sup>th</sup> week on Billboard&#8217;s album chart, it became the first title ever to be certified RIAA 20X multi-Platinum (<span class=\"xn-chron\">October 30, 1984</span>) and became the first title ever to be certified more than RIAA 30X multi-Platinum in 2015 and since has been certified at 34x Multi-Platinum.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>\n<p>Worldwide, <i>Thriller</i> went to #1 in practically every country in the world, including the UK, <span class=\"xn-location\">France</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Italy</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Australia</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Denmark</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Belgium</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">South Africa</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Spain</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Ireland</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">New Zealand</span>, <span class=\"xn-location\">Canada</span> and apartheid <span class=\"xn-location\">South Africa</span> and remains the biggest selling album of all time with sales in excess of 100 million.&nbsp; Seven tracks from the album became Top 10 singles, and three, &#8220;Beat It,&#8221; &#8220;<span class=\"xn-person\">Billie Jean</span>,&#8221; and &#8220;Thriller,&#8221; went No. 1.&nbsp;</p>\n<p>The music on <i>Thriller</i> was so singular for the time it defied any definition of rock, pop or soul that had gone before. &#8220;Beat It&#8221; was a new kind of pop-rock hybrid and demolished the longstanding segregation between black and white music with <span class=\"xn-person\">Eddie Van Halen&#8217;s</span> incendiary guitar. On &#8220;The Girl Is Mine,&#8221; two men, one white and one Black, banter about the same girl. On the same album were songs like the African-rooted &#8220;<span class=\"xn-person\">Wanna Be Startin</span>&#8216; Somethin'&#8221; and the rhythm and blues-based &#8220;<span class=\"xn-person\">Billie Jean</span>.&#8221; &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>\n<p>Michael set out to revolutionize how to tell a story and entertain on a grand scale through film but faced roadblocks. Despite the luscious cinematography, dramatic narrative and spectacular choreography of &#8220;<span class=\"xn-person\">Billie Jean</span>,&#8221; a fledgling MTV, which was programming white rock artists almost exclusively, refused to play it. Epic persisted. Once the wall came crashing down, MTV&#8217;s ratings soared and a door was opened for a generation of African American artists. &#8220;He was MTV&#8217;s <span class=\"xn-person\">Jackie Robinson</span>,&#8221; said cultural critic Touré.</p>\n<p>Next, came the unforgettable short film for &#8220;Beat It,&#8221; which featured Michael bringing two gangs together through the power of music and dance. And then there was &#8220;Thriller.&#8221; Premiered at the AVCO Theatre in <span class=\"xn-location\">Los Angeles</span> in 1983, it sold out every night for three weeks. No other video before or since has generated such excitement and has such a hold on our attention, such that 40 years later we all share it as a collective memory and it remains the only music video to be inducted into the elite National Film Registry by the Library of Congress.</p>\n<p>Music from <i>Thriller</i> and Michael&#8217;s other hit albums is featured in the hit <span class=\"xn-location\">Las Vegas</span> show <b>Michael Jackson ONE</b>, at the Michael Jackson ONE Theater in Mandalay Bay Resorts and Casino and in the acclaimed Broadway musical <b>MJ The Musical </b>at the Neil Simon Theatre in <span class=\"xn-location\">New York</span>, which was nominated for 10 Tony Awards last week.
